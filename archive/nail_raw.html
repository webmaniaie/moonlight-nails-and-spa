Our Story

A Place Where
You Feel Pampered

The Nail Parlour started as a dream between two sisters who wanted to bring a different kind of nail salon to Carlow — one built on warmth, cleanliness, and genuine care. At 1 Kennedy Street, in the heart of town, we've grown from a single workspace into a beloved community spot where everyone is welcome, no appointment necessary.

We're known for meticulous attention to detail (ask anyone about Helen's delicate nail-art work), a calming, organised space, and complimentary refreshments while you're seated. Whether you're here for a Spa Pedicure, a fresh set of Gel/BIAB, or a bold nail-art creation, you leave feeling pampered, not rushed.
